About Kay

Kay from:

With a name longer than ten characters I've held lots of nicknames in the past; these days I mostly go by Kay. I like to think of myself as creative and fun, and I love working in a team! I am addicted to the tingling sensation I feel every time I get my hands on a new, inspiring, project!

I consider everything that surrounds us to be designed in a certain way, and I firmly believe that design thinking and practice can make a good idea reach its full potential. The desire to help people make the most out of what they do feeds my passion for high quality, efficient yet beautiful work. With a background in graphic design and software development, I enjoy using mix-media tools to achieve the most fitting result.
